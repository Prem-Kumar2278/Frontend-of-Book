## Key Concepts

Throughout this module, you'll develop understanding of:

- **Sim2Real Transfer**: How synthetic data bridges the gap between simulation and real-world performance
- **Spatial Intelligence**: How robots achieve spatial awareness and maintain accurate environmental models
- **Motion Intelligence**: How perception information guides navigation and locomotion decisions
- **Humanoid Constraints**: How bipedal movement requirements influence navigation planning
