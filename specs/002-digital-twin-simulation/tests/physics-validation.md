# Physics Simulation Concepts Content Validation Checklist

Use this checklist to validate the technical accuracy and completeness of Physics Simulation Foundations content.

## Core Physics Simulation Concepts

- [ ] Clear explanation of gravity modeling and its effects in simulation
- [ ] Proper definition of collision detection and response mechanisms

- [ ] Understanding of physics simulation fidelity vs complexity trade-offs

- [ ] Learner can explain the role of gravity in physics simulation
